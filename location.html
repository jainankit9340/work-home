Location

KORAMANGALA

Koramangala is known for its cosmopolitan culture and charm. It is the startup hub of the country and also has a high concentration of restaurants, presently around 700. Having close proximity to MG Road, Indiranagar, HSR Layout, Jayanagar, Richmond Town, Electronic City and Whitefield - Koramangala is a retail hub, a shopper’s paradise, a foodie’s delight and the most desired work location in Bangalore.

With 100 Feet Road and the 80 Feet Road being the arterial connecting corridors to several areas, Koramangala is the epicentre of South Bangalore. WORK HOME is strategically located on the 1st Cross of 5th Block, the link between these 2 roads. This street in the recent years is more popularly known for the number of restaurants, cafes, bars and co working spaces in high concentration.

The neighbourhood is also dotted with prestigious landmarks and the best names in retail including designer brands in apparel, jewellery like Tanishq, St. John’s Hospital, Jyothi Nivas College, Apollo Spectra Hospitals, Cult and Mind Fit, Nature’s Basket, Commune Co Works, Incubex Co working Space, William Penn, Bblunt Salon. It is marked by the presence of some known FnB outlets like Asia Kitchen by Mainland China, Fava Bistro, Chianti, Om Made Cafe, Gillys, Reservoire, Nagarjuna, Bon South, Koramangala Social, Sukh Sagar, Truffles, Corner House and Mc Donald’s.
