Location

KORAMANGALA

Koramangala is known for its cosmopolitan culture and charm. It is the startup hub of the country and in the past decade has become a food paradise, having a high concentration of restaurants, highest in the country. Having close proximity to MG Road, Indiranagar, HSR Layout, Jayanagar, Richmond Town, Electronic City & Whitefield- Koramangala is a retail hub, a shopper’s paradise, a foodie’s delight & the most desired work location in Bangalore.

With 100 feet Road & the 80 Feet Road being the arterial connecting corridors to several areas, Koramangala is the epicentre of South Bangalore. WORKHOME is strategically located on the 1st Cross of 5th Block, the link between these 2 roads. This street in recent years is more popularly known for the number of restaurants, cafes, bars and co-working spaces in high concentration.

The neighbourhood is also dotted with prestigious landmarks and the best names in retail including designer brands in apparel, jewellery like Tanishq, St. John’s Hospital, Jyothi Nivas College, Apollo Spectra Hospital, Nature’s Basket, 315 Work Avenue, Clayworks, William Penn. It is marked by the presence of some known FnB outlets like Pizza Express, Popeye’s Chianti, Gillys, Reservoire, Daily Sushi, Nagarjuna, Koramangala Social, Sukh Sagar, Truffles, Corner House, KFC & Mc. Donald’s.
